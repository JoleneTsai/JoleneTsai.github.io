課程概述 Course Description

本門課將介紹Android智慧型手機應用程式開發軟體APP Inventor 2，透過學習如何使用APP Inventor 2循序漸進學習製作簡易手機應用程式，協助成大醫院系統的APP之製作。並透過至成大醫院進行實際志工服務，了解服務需求者可能所需之應用程式功能，培養同學之觀察與問題解決能力。
This course will introduce the Android smartphone application development software APP Inventor 2. By learning how to use APP Inventor 2, you will learn to create simple mobile applications step by step, and assist in the production of APPs for Chengda Hospital system. Through actual volunteer services at National Cheng Kung University Hospital, students can understand the application functions that may be needed by those in need of services, and cultivate students' observation and problem-solving abilities.
